# ⚔️ PlanYourDay — Craft Your Quest

> A Minecraft-themed daily planner, diary, to-do list & reminder app.
> **Installable as a free app — no App Store needed!**

🌐 **Live:** https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/planyourday

---

## 📲 How to Install (Android)

1. Open the link above in **Chrome** on your Android phone
2. Tap the **3-dot menu** → **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. Tap **Add** — done!

The app now lives on your home screen, works **offline**, and feels like a native app. Free forever. ⚔️

## 🍎 How to Install (iPhone)

1. Open the link in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share button** (box with arrow)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add**

---

## 🎮 Features

- **Quest Board** — Today / Weekly / Epic task tiers with XP rewards
- **Adventurer's Log** — Daily diary with mood tracking
- **Torches** — Named reminders with time alerts
- **RPG System** — XP bar, level ups, 8 achievement badges
- **Inventory** — Live stats, health hearts, hunger bar
- **100% Offline** — Works without internet after first load
- **Private** — All data stays on your device, never uploaded
